About Us

Who are we?

Established in 2022, Cyclo Domestique is a family business with 1 store located in south west Endgland. Cyclo Domestique's store feature Road, Mountain, Kids and Electric Bike and offer a wide range of products and services to all types of cyclists, from children learning to ride their first bike, through to performance athletes.

We remain committed to inspire more people to get out and ride a bike. For us, it's about much more than just selling bicycles â€” we're here to help people achieve their cycling goals. As we've grown, our family values have helped us build a strong team ethos and keep us focused on delivering an excellent experience for our customers.

We believe passionately that to get more people making cycling part of their lives, you've got to make it convenient and easy. So, in our Cyclo Domestique store, you can test a bike, before getting your own bike or if you need it repaired and spares and accessories. We also believe in leading by example and inspiring new cyclists to give it a go. In the end, it's all about going the extra mile, both for our customers and each other.

Our community work

We love cycling! And right from the start, we have sought to share our passion for two-wheeled travel with our local communities. We also deliver community maintenance classes and bike events. For beginners' we host road rides every Wednesday at 6pm and MTB ride in Shorne Woods every Friday at 5pm. No booking needed just come and ride with us.

We also enjoy offering proactive support to our local charity partners by participating in fundraising events, offer event support and raise awareness.
